Biography

Hello! This is LiangLu, a Ph.D candidate in traffic engineering at School Of Transportion and Logistics, SouthWest JiaoTong University, in chengdu city, working on connected vehicles and autonomous vehicles driving group led by Prof. fangfangzheng. My research focused on modeling the mixed traffic flow with both human drivers vehicles (HV) and autonomous vehicles (AV), estimating traffic state and optimaling traffic system by considering AV control strategies.
